RoProfile - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information Collected

RoProfile does not collect, store, or sell personal information.

The bot only processes the information necessary to execute its commands.

2. Roblox Data

RoProfile displays publicly available Roblox profile information obtained from Roblox's public services.

3. Discord Data

RoProfile only accesses the information required to operate within Discord, such as interaction data needed to execute slash commands.

4. Data Storage

RoProfile does not permanently store user personal data.

5. Third-Party Services

The bot relies on Discord and Roblox services, each governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.

7. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the developer through Discord.
